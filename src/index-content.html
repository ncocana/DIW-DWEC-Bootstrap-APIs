Discover some of our Pokemon

Kyurem

Legendary Pokémon that awaits the hero who compensates for the emptiness of his ice body with truth and ideals. It produces intense icy energy within it, and any leakage causes its body to freeze. Mewtwo

Mewtwo is extremely skilled with its psychic powers: it can fly through levitation, communicate telepathically, reflect attacks such as hyper beam, block other Pokémon's special abilities, and take command of another living creature's mind through hypnosis.

Lucario

Lucario is a Fighting/Steel-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. It is the evolution of Riolu and can Mega Evolve into Mega-Lucario.

Rayquaza

Rayquaza is a legendary dragon/flying type Pokémon introduced in the third generation. It is the mascot of Pokémon Emerald. He is part of the creator trio along with Groudon and Kyogre. From Pokémon Omega Ruby and Pokémon Alpha Sapphire it can Mega Evolve into Mega-Rayquaza.
