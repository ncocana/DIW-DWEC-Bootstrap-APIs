All pokemon video games

Pokemon Scarlet and Purple

Set in a new region, players embark on a journey to become Pokémon Champions while uncovering the mysteries behind legendary Pokémon Scarlet and Purple, who hold the balance of power in the region.

Pokemon Let's Go Pikachu and Pokémon Let's Go Eevee

These games are modern remakes of Pokémon Yellow Version, where players journey through the Kanto region with either Pikachu or Eevee as their partner Pokémon, capturing and battling Pokémon in a quest to become the Pokémon Champion.

Pokémon Legends: Arceus

Set in the ancient Sinnoh region, players explore the open world, capturing Pokémon and uncovering the origin of the Pokémon world's first Pokédex. They embark on a quest to study and document the behaviors of Pokémon, including the legendary Arceus.

Pokemon UNITE

A multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game where teams of Pokémon compete against each other in strategic, five-versus-five battles, capturing wild Pokémon and defeating opponents to score points and claim victory.


Pokemon Sword and Shield

Players journey through the Galar region, capturing and battling Pokémon to become the Champion while thwarting the plans of the villainous Team Yell and uncovering the mystery behind the Legendary Pokémon Zacian and Zamazenta.

Pokemon Brilliant Diamont and Pokémon Shining Pearl

Remakes of the original Pokémon Diamond and Pearl games, players explore the Sinnoh region, capturing and battling Pokémon on their quest to become the Champion and uncover the secrets of the Legendary Pokémon Dialga and Palkia.

Pokemon GO

A mobile augmented reality game where players explore the real world to find and capture Pokémon, engage in battles at Gyms, and participate in events with other players.

Pokemon X y Y

Players explore the Kalos region, capturing and battling Pokémon while thwarting the plans of the villainous Team Flare and uncovering the mysteries of Mega Evolution and the Legendary Pokémon Xerneas and Yveltal.


New Pokemon Snap

Players take on the role of a Pokémon photographer, exploring various islands to capture photos of Pokémon in their natural habitats and uncover the mysteries of the Illumina phenomenon.

Pokemon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon

Enhanced versions of Pokémon Sun and Moon, players journey through the Alola region to become the Champion while facing new challenges and encountering alternate forms of Legendary Pokémon Solgaleo and Lunala.

Detective Pikachu

Players take on the role of Tim Goodman, teaming up with a talking Pikachu to solve mysteries and uncover the whereabouts of Tim's missing father in Ryme City.

Pokemon Black and White

Players journey through the Unova region, capturing and battling Pokémon while challenging the Pokémon League and thwarting the plans of the villainous Team Plasma to liberate Pokémon from human control.
